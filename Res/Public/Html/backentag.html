Throughout the summer months, we gather on the first Sunday of each month for our International Baking Day. This special tradition brings together friends and family to celebrate the art of baking with a focus on bread, desserts, and pizza from around the world.

Each session features unique recipes and techniques shared by participants from different cultural backgrounds. From Italian focaccia to Turkish pide, German pretzels to American pies - our baking days are filled with delicious aromas, laughter, and the joy of creating something with our hands.

Join us for our next gathering and experience the universal language of baking that connects people across cultures and generations!
